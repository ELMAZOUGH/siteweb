About Me

Test Automation Engineer

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Yassine El Mazoughi, and I am an ISTQB-certified Test Automation Engineer with extensive experience in software testing. Throughout my career, I have honed my expertise in test automation, quality assurance, and test process management. I have worked on a variety of projects, including CRM systems and core banking software, where I designed and implemented automated test scripts to ensure the quality, performance, and reliability of applications. With a methodical and detail-oriented approach, I specialize in identifying anomalies and continuously enhancing the quality of software products. Passionate about emerging technologies, I constantly explore innovative techniques to optimize testing processes. My goal is to deliver high-quality software while minimizing development time and costs.
